Scheduler
Scheduler is a website that syncs with Google Calendar to help you find times when you and all your friends are available. When you create an event, you can fill in all important information such as title, description, and even guests. Once the event has been submitted, Scheduler automatically creates the event and pushes it to your Google account.

Scheduling Conflicts
When you find and add your friends to an event, all prospective attendees will have their calendars put into an algorithm to determine overlapping busy times. From there, you can select the time of the event based on their schedules and this allows you to be sure that everyone is free simultaneously.
Profile Interface
Scheduler will have you create an account with your associated Google Calendar to know when you are available. You can customize your account with profile pictures and usernames. Add friends for convenient access to see when they are busy and view their profiles.
